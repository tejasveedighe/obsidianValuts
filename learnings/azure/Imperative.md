
---
- Imperative way is the opposite of [[Declarative]] way of creating resources in Azure.
- The imperative method is to directly send instructions that are executed at once, it is done iteratively.
- Imperative method can be error prone, can't be verified, not controlled.
- It is used for doing small dirty operations.
- Is is used by [[azure/Azure CLI]] and [[azure/Azure Powershell]] tools.
