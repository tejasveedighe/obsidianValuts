- Introduction -
	- Reservation policy refers to the affirmative action taken by the government of India to support the minorities of India.
	- Reservation is part of our constitution that gives reservation in jobs, education and provides financial support to the weaker sections of society.
	- The idea for reservation policy started during the time of British Raj over India. 
	- The primary beneficiaries are SC and STs.
