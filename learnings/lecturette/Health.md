- What is the topic?
	- Health is defined as the entire well being of a person.
	- There are different types of health-
		1. Physical - Exercise, nutrition, and medical care
		2. Mental - Psychological wellness, emotional resilience, stress management
		3. Societal - Interpersonal skills, relations
		4. Environmental - Surrounding cleanliness, climate
- What is the current status of Health in India?
	- A new health crisis of mental health has emerged and has been drastically increasing through out the world.
	- TB has been a primary health problem in India and is still prevailing.
	- India has increased the mortality rate of under 5 children and improved the sex ratio comparing the data from 2021.
	- Cancer is the largest cause of death in India and has new 2.3 million cases.
- What can be done to change the current status of health in India?
	- Starting from cancer treatment, the govt must appoint more percentage to healthcare and medical research. Currently 1.3% of GDP is allocated to fund medical in India and must be added. Also adding restrictions of cancer producing food items and consumables like tabacco.
		- National Programme for Prevention of Cancer, Diabetes, Cardiovascular Diseases and Stroke is the govt initiative.
	- For mental health the govt should create policies that rewards the corporate companies that keep their employees health as a focus.
		- Some companies that have programmes that make their employees move for 10 minutes and 10 minutes for guided meditation can drop the mental health problems by a lot. Such companies should get tax benefits.
	- The govt must add more medical facilities in Govt Hospitals as it still lacks the same equipment as the private hospitals. Schemes to increase the count of govt employeed doctors and nurses should be added. Current 10 doctors and 20 nurses are there for 10,000 people.
- Challenges in changing these problems -
	- Tax benefits for companies that are already good at reducing the taxes might just not do anything to get the tax benefits but show it on paper.
	- The training of doctors and nurses is hard as the competition is very high.
